Breakfast: Citrus Greek Yogurt with Pomegranate & Almonds
Show/Hide Breakfast Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups full-fat Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 cup pomegranate arils (or 1 pomegranate)
  • ¼ cup slivered almonds

Recipe Directions

  1. Spoon the yogurt into two bowls.

  3. Enjoy immediately.

  • Fats: ~12 g
Dinner: Herbed Baked Cod with Tomato-Olive Relish, Quinoa Salad & Roasted Asparagus
(Leftovers for Tuesday Lunch)
Show/Hide Dinner Recipe

Ingredients

Baked Cod

  • 2 cod fillets (6 oz each)
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tsp chopped fresh basil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Tomato-Olive Relish

  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ¼ cup Kalamata olives, chopped
  • 1 tbsp capers
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Quinoa Salad

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water or low-sodium broth
  • ½ red onion, finely chopped
  • ½ cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Roasted Asparagus

  • 1 bunch asparagus (about 12 spears), trimmed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Recipe Directions

  1. Prepare Cod: Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Place cod in a baking dish; drizzle with olive oil, lemon juice, add garlic, basil, salt, and pepper. Bake for 12–15 minutes until flaky.
  2. Make Relish: Combine cherry tomatoes, olives, capers, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Cook Quinoa: Bring water or broth to a boil; add quinoa, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes. Fluff and mix with red onion and parsley, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Roast Asparagus: Toss asparagus with olive oil, salt, and pepper; roast on a baking sheet for 10–12 minutes.
  5. Assemble: Serve cod topped with tomato-olive relish, alongside quinoa salad and roasted asparagus. Reserve extra for Tuesday’s lunch.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~520 kcal
  • Protein: ~40 g
  • Carbs: ~42 g
  • Fats: ~16 g

Lunch: Leftover Monday Dinner
Dinner: Greek Chicken Souvlaki with Tzatziki & Greek Salad
(Leftovers for Wednesday Lunch)
Show/Hide Dinner Recipe

Ingredients

Chicken Souvlaki

  • 2 chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Tzatziki

  • 1 cup full-fat Greek yogurt
  • ½ cucumber, grated and squeezed dry
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tsp chopped fresh dill
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • Salt, to taste

Greek Salad

  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ small cucumber, diced
  • ¼ red onion, thinly sliced
  • 10 Kalamata olives, pitted
  • ¼ cup crumbled feta
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp dried oregano
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Recipe Directions

  1. Marinate Chicken: Toss chicken cubes with olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper; marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Grill Souvlaki: Thread chicken onto skewers and grill over medium-high heat for 8–10 minutes until cooked through; then remove from skewers.
  3. Prepare Tzatziki: Combine all tzatziki ingredients in a bowl and mix well.
  4. Make Salad: Toss cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, olives, and feta with olive oil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  5. Assemble: Serve grilled chicken with a side of tzatziki and Greek salad. Reserve extra for Wednesday’s lunch.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~550 kcal
  • Protein: ~40 g
  • Carbs: ~30 g
  • Fats: ~25 g

Breakfast: Spinach & Feta Egg Muffins
Show/Hide Breakfast Recipe

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • ½ cup diced red bell pepper
  • ½ cup crumbled feta
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Recipe Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Beat eggs with salt and pepper; stir in spinach, bell pepper, and feta.
  3. Pour mixture into a greased muffin tin.
  4. Bake for 20 minutes until set.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~280 kcal
  • Protein: ~18 g
  • Carbs: ~6 g
  • Fats: ~20 g
Lunch: Leftover Tuesday Dinner
Dinner: Mediterranean Roasted Vegetable Pasta with Spinach & Feta
(Leftovers for Thursday Lunch)
Show/Hide Dinner Recipe

Ingredients

  • 8 oz whole-grain pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 small zucchini, sliced
  • ½ small eggplant, cubed
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • ¼ cup crumbled feta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • Fresh basil, for garnish (optional)

Recipe Directions

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions; drain.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil and sauté garlic for 1 minute.
  3. Add cherry tomatoes, zucchini, and eggplant; cook until tender (about 5–7 minutes).
  4. Stir in fresh spinach until wilted, then toss in the cooked pasta and crumbled feta.
  5. Season with salt and pepper; garnish with fresh basil. Reserve extra for Thursday’s lunch.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~600 kcal
  • Protein: ~42 g
  • Carbs: ~55 g
  • Fats: ~18 g

Lunch: Leftover Tuesday Dinner
Dinner: Mediterranean Stuffed Bell Peppers with Couscous & Feta
(Leftovers for Friday Lunch)
Show/Hide Dinner Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 large bell peppers, halved and seeded
  • 1 cup cooked couscous
  • ½ cup canned chickpeas, rinsed
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • ½ cup diced zucchini
  • ¼ cup chopped red onion
  • ½ cup crumbled feta
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Recipe Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix couscous, chickpeas, diced tomatoes, zucchini, red onion, feta, parsley, olive oil, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Fill each bell pepper half with the mixture and arrange in a baking dish.
  4. Bake for 25–30 minutes until the peppers are tender and the filling is heated through.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~480 kcal
  • Protein: ~18 g
  • Carbs: ~65 g
  • Fats: ~16 g
