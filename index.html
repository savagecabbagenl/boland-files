Breakfast: Greek Yogurt Parfait
Show/Hide Breakfast Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups full-fat Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • ½ cup walnuts, roughly chopped
  • 1 cup mixed fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • ½ cup granola

Recipe Directions

  1. Divide the Greek yogurt equally between two bowls.
  2. Drizzle with honey and sprinkle with walnuts.
  3. Top with fresh berries and granola.
  4. Enjoy immediately.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~300 kcal
  • Protein: ~20 g
  • Carbs: ~35 g
  • Fats: ~10 g
Dinner: Baked Lemon-Herb Chicken with Quinoa Tabbouleh & Roasted Vegetables
(Leftovers for Tuesday Lunch)
Show/Hide Dinner Recipe

Ingredients

Baked Lemon-Herb Chicken

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (6–8 oz each)
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Quinoa Tabbouleh

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water or low-sodium broth
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 medium cucumber, diced
  • ½ red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • ¼ cup fresh mint, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • (Optional: ½ cup crumbled feta)

Roasted Vegetables

  • Assorted vegetables (zucchini, bell pepper, eggplant), cubed
  • Olive oil, salt & pepper

Recipe Directions

  1. Marinate & Bake Chicken: Marinate chicken in olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper. Bake or grill until cooked through (165°F/74°C) then slice.
  2. Prepare Quinoa & Tabbouleh: Cook quinoa in water or broth. Toss with tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, parsley, mint, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Stir in feta if using.
  3. Roast Vegetables: Toss cubed vegetables in olive oil, salt, and pepper; roast in a preheated 425°F (220°C) oven until tender and lightly caramelized.
  4. Assemble: Plate the sliced chicken over a bed of tabbouleh with roasted vegetables on the side. Reserve extra for Tuesday’s lunch.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~500 kcal
  • Protein: ~40 g
  • Carbs: ~40 g
  • Fats: ~18 g

Breakfast: Mediterranean Veggie Omelette
Show/Hide Breakfast Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup spinach, roughly chopped
  • ½ cup diced tomatoes
  • ¼ cup crumbled feta
  • 6–8 Kalamata olives, sliced
  • Fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • 1 tsp olive oil

Recipe Directions

  1. Beat eggs with salt and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Heat olive oil in a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Sauté spinach and tomatoes until just wilted.
  3. Pour eggs over veggies; sprinkle feta, olives, and basil.
  4. Cook until eggs are set; fold and serve.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~320 kcal
  • Protein: ~22 g
  • Carbs: ~8 g
  • Fats: ~22 g
Lunch: Leftover Monday Dinner
Dinner: Baked Salmon with Olive-Caper Salsa & Roasted Mediterranean Vegetables
(Leftovers for Wednesday Lunch)
Show/Hide Dinner Recipe

Ingredients

Baked Salmon

  • 2 salmon fillets (≈6 oz each)
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • 2 lemon slices (plus extra juice if desired)

Olive-Caper Salsa

  • ¼ cup pitted Kalamata olives, chopped
  • 2 tbsp capers, drained and chopped
  • ¼ small red onion, finely diced
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil

Roasted Vegetables

  • Assorted veggies: eggplant, zucchini, red bell pepper, cherry tomatoes
  • Olive oil, salt & pepper

Recipe Directions

  1. Bake the Salmon: Brush salmon with olive oil, season with salt and pepper, top with lemon slices, and bake at 425°F (220°C) for 12–15 minutes until flaky.
  2. Prepare the Salsa: Combine olives, capers, red onion, parsley, lemon juice, and olive oil in a bowl.
  3. Roast the Vegetables: Toss vegetables in olive oil, salt, and pepper; roast until tender.
  4. Assemble: Serve salmon topped with salsa alongside roasted vegetables. Reserve extra for Wednesday’s lunch.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~550 kcal
  • Protein: ~35 g
  • Carbs: ~30 g
  • Fats: ~30 g

Lunch: Leftover Tuesday Dinner
Dinner: Mediterranean Chicken Thighs with Chickpea Stew & Couscous
(Leftovers for Thursday Lunch)
Show/Hide Dinner Recipe

Ingredients

Chicken Thighs

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Mixed herbs (thyme, oregano), salt & pepper

Chickpea Stew

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup spinach, roughly chopped
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Couscous

  • 1 cup whole-wheat couscous
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • Chopped parsley
  • Salt & a drizzle of olive oil

Recipe Directions

  1. Roast Chicken Thighs: Marinate thighs with olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, herbs, salt, and pepper. Roast at 400°F (200°C) for 35–40 minutes until crispy and cooked through.
  2. Prepare Chickpea Stew: Sauté diced onion in olive oil until translucent. Add garlic, diced tomatoes, chickpeas, spinach, and smoked paprika; simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  3. Make Couscous: Pour boiling water over couscous in a bowl, cover for 5 minutes, then fluff with a fork and stir in parsley, salt, and olive oil.
  4. Assemble: Serve chicken thighs over chickpea stew with a side of couscous. Reserve extra stew for Thursday’s lunch.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~600 kcal
  • Protein: ~45 g
  • Carbs: ~50 g
  • Fats: ~20 g
Snack: Baba Ganoush with Pita Chips
Show/Hide Snack Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 medium eggplant
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • Whole-grain pita bread, cut into chips

Recipe Directions

  1. Roast the eggplant whole at 400°F (200°C) until soft (about 40 minutes). Cool, then scoop out the flesh.
  2. Blend the eggplant flesh with garlic, tahini, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper until smooth.
  3. Bake pita chips until crisp or serve as is.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~200 kcal
  • Protein: ~5 g
  • Carbs: ~25 g
  • Fats: ~10 g

Show/Hide Breakfast Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado

Dinner: Greek-Style Fish Stew with Tomatoes & Olives
(Leftovers for Friday Lunch)
Show/Hide Dinner Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 white fish fillets (cod or halibut, ≈6 oz each)
  • 1 potato, cubed; 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • ½ cup Kalamata olives, pitted
  • 1 tbsp capers
  • A splash of white wine
  • Fresh oregano and dill
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Recipe Directions

  1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until soft. Add potato, carrot, and diced tomatoes; simmer for 10 minutes.
  2. Add white wine, olives, capers, salt, pepper, and herbs. Nestle the fish fillets into the stew and simmer until cooked (8–10 minutes).
  3. Garnish with extra oregano and dill. Reserve extra for Friday’s lunch.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~500 kcal
  • Protein: ~35 g
  • Carbs: ~40 g
  • Fats: ~15 g

Lunch: Leftover Thursday Dinner
Dinner: Vegetable Moussaka with Lentils & Eggplant
(Leftovers for Saturday Lunch)
Show/Hide Dinner Recipe

Ingredients

Moussaka Layers

  • 2 eggplants, sliced thinly
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup cooked lentils
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tsp cinnamon & ½ tsp allspice
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil

Béchamel Sauce

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 ½ cups low-fat milk
  • A pinch of nutmeg
  • Salt & pepper

Recipe Directions

  1. Prepare Vegetables: Salt eggplant and zucchini slices lightly; let drain for 15 minutes. Rinse, pat dry, then sauté lightly in olive oil until softened.
  2. Make Lentil-Tomato Sauce: Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until translucent. Add diced tomatoes, cooked lentils, cinnamon, allspice, salt, and pepper. Simmer until thickened (about 15 minutes).
  3. Prepare Béchamel: In a saucepan, melt butter, whisk in flour until smooth, then slowly add milk while whisking. Add nutmeg, salt, and pepper; cook until thick.
  4. Layer & Bake: In a baking dish, layer the sautéed vegetables with the lentil-tomato sauce. Pour béchamel sauce over the top. Bake in a preheated 375°F (190°C) oven for 30–35 minutes until bubbly and golden.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~550 kcal
  • Protein: ~25 g
  • Carbs: ~60 g
  • Fats: ~20 g
