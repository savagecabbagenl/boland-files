Revised Daily Meal Plan

Total Calories: Approximately 2,000 kcal

Breakfast – Greek Yogurt Parfait (≈400 kcal)

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • ½ cup mixed berries (blueberries, strawberries, raspberries)
  • 2 tablespoons granola
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon chia seeds

Benefits: High in protein and antioxidants to start your day strong.

Lunch – Quinoa Chicken Salad (≈600 kcal)

  • 4–5 oz grilled chicken breast
  • ½ cup cooked quinoa
  • A large handful of mixed greens (spinach, arugula, romaine)
  • Cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red bell pepper
  • ¼ avocado (sliced)
  • Lemon-olive oil dressing (lemon juice, extra virgin olive oil, salt, and pepper)

Benefits: A balanced mix of lean protein, healthy fats, and complex carbohydrates.

Snack – Minimal Prep Apple & Almonds (≈150 kcal)

  • 1 medium apple, sliced
  • A small handful (about 10–15) of raw almonds

Benefits: Quick, nutritious, and portable; no cooking required.

Dinner – Mediterranean-Inspired Plate with Variety (≈850 kcal)

This dinner offers variety while reducing the eggplant quantity by half compared to previous plans.

Option 1: Grilled Salmon Plate

  • 5–6 oz salmon fillet, grilled with herbs and lemon
  • Roasted Brussels sprouts and sweet potato mash
  • Side: A small portion of grilled eggplant (50% of the previous amount)

Option 2: Grilled Tofu & Veggie Bowl (Plant-Based Option)

  • Marinated tofu cubes grilled to perfection
  • A colorful mix of roasted vegetables (broccoli, carrots, red bell pepper)
  • Small serving of grilled eggplant (reduced by 50%)
  • Served over a small portion of whole-grain couscous

Benefits: Provides varied flavors and nutrients while keeping eggplant usage modest.

Nutritional Breakdown

  • Carbohydrates: ~45% (~900 kcal)
  • Protein: ~30% (~600 kcal)
  • Fat: ~25% (~500 kcal)
