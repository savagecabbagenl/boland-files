Monday

Breakfast: Mediterranean Overnight Chia Pudding
Show/Hide Breakfast Recipe

Ingredients

  • ½ cup chia seeds
  • 2 cups almond milk
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup mixed fresh berries
  • 2 tbsp Greek yogurt (swirled on top)

Recipe Directions

  1. In a bowl or jar, combine chia seeds, almond milk, honey, and vanilla extract.
  2. Mix well and refrigerate overnight.
  3. Before serving, stir and top with fresh berries and a dollop of Greek yogurt.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~310 kcal
  • Protein: ~10 g
  • Carbs: ~35 g
  • Fats: ~12 g
Dinner: Baked Lemon-Herb Tilapia with Couscous Salad & Steamed Broccoli
(Leftovers for Tuesday Lunch)
Show/Hide Dinner Recipe

Ingredients

Baked Tilapia

  • 2 tilapia fillets (6 oz each)
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tsp chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Couscous Salad

  • 1 cup couscous
  • 2 cups water or low-sodium broth
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ small cucumber, diced
  • ¼ small red onion, finely chopped
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Steamed Broccoli

  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • A pinch of salt

Recipe Directions

  1. Prepare Tilapia: Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Place tilapia in a baking dish, drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice, add minced garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper. Bake for 12–15 minutes until flaky.
  2. Make Couscous Salad: Bring water or broth to a boil, add couscous, cover, and let steam for 5 minutes. Fluff and mix with tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, parsley, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Steam Broccoli: Steam broccoli florets until crisp-tender (about 5 minutes).
  4. Assemble: Plate tilapia with a generous serving of couscous salad and a side of steamed broccoli. Reserve extra for Tuesday’s lunch.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~520 kcal
  • Protein: ~40 g
  • Carbs: ~42 g
  • Fats: ~16 g

Lunch: Leftover Monday Dinner
Dinner: Greek Chicken Souvlaki with Tzatziki & Greek Salad
(Leftovers for Wednesday Lunch)
Show/Hide Dinner Recipe

Ingredients

Chicken Souvlaki

  • 2 chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Tzatziki

  • 1 cup full-fat Greek yogurt
  • ½ cucumber, grated and squeezed dry
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tsp chopped fresh dill
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • Salt, to taste

Greek Salad

  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ small cucumber, diced
  • ¼ red onion, thinly sliced
  • 10 Kalamata olives, pitted
  • ¼ cup crumbled feta
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Recipe Directions

  1. Marinate Chicken: Toss chicken cubes with olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper; let marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Grill Souvlaki: Thread chicken onto skewers and grill over medium-high heat until cooked through (about 8–10 minutes total), then remove from skewers.
  3. Make Tzatziki: Combine Greek yogurt, grated cucumber, garlic, dill, lemon juice, and salt in a bowl.
  4. Prepare Salad: Toss tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, olives, and feta with olive oil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  5. Assemble: Serve chicken with a side of tzatziki and Greek salad. Reserve extra for Wednesday’s lunch.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~550 kcal
  • Protein: ~40 g
  • Carbs: ~30 g
  • Fats: ~25 g

Breakfast: Spinach & Feta Egg Muffins
Show/Hide Breakfast Recipe

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • ½ cup crumbled feta
  • ½ cup diced red bell pepper
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Recipe Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Beat eggs with salt and pepper; stir in spinach, feta, and red bell pepper.
  3. Pour mixture into greased muffin tin cups.
  4. Bake for 20 minutes until set.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~280 kcal
  • Protein: ~18 g
  • Carbs: ~6 g
  • Fats: ~20 g
Lunch: Leftover Tuesday Dinner
Dinner: Easy Shrimp & Veggie Pasta
(Leftovers for Thursday Lunch)
Show/Hide Dinner Recipe

Ingredients

Shrimp & Veggie Pasta

  • 8 oz whole-grain pasta
  • 12 large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Recipe Directions

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté garlic for 1 minute.
  3. Add shrimp and cook until pink (about 3–4 minutes); add cherry tomatoes and spinach, then drizzle with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Toss in pasta and mix until well combined. Reserve extra for Thursday’s lunch.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~600 kcal
  • Protein: ~42 g
  • Carbs: ~55 g
  • Fats: ~18 g

Lunch: Leftover Wednesday Dinner
Dinner: Chickpea & Vegetable Stew with Brown Rice
(Leftovers for Friday Lunch)
Show/Hide Dinner Recipe

Ingredients

Chickpea & Vegetable Stew

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 oz)
  • 1 small zucchini, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small eggplant, diced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Brown Rice

  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 2 cups water
  • A pinch of salt

Recipe Directions

  1. Prepare Stew: In a large pot, heat olive oil and sauté onion and garlic until soft. Add eggplant, zucchini, and red bell pepper; cook for 5 minutes.
  2. Add diced tomatoes, chickpeas, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper; simmer for 15–20 minutes until vegetables are tender and flavors meld.
  3. Cook Rice: Bring water to a boil, add brown rice and salt, then simmer covered for about 40–45 minutes until tender.
  4. Assemble: Serve the vegetable stew over brown rice. Reserve extra stew for Friday’s lunch.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~530 kcal
  • Protein: ~16 g
  • Carbs: ~75 g
  • Fats: ~15 g
