Breakfast: Greek Yogurt Parfait
Show/Hide Breakfast Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups full-fat Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • ½ cup walnuts, roughly chopped
  • 1 cup mixed fresh berries
  • ½ cup granola

Recipe Directions

  1. Divide the yogurt evenly between two bowls.
  2. Drizzle each with honey and sprinkle walnuts over the top.
  3. Add fresh berries and finish with granola.
  4. Enjoy immediately.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~300 kcal
  • Protein: ~20 g
  • Carbs: ~35 g
  • Fats: ~10 g
Dinner: Baked Lemon-Herb Chicken with Quinoa Tabbouleh & Roasted Vegetables
(Leftovers for Tuesday Lunch)
Show/Hide Dinner Recipe

Ingredients

Baked Lemon-Herb Chicken

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (6–8 oz each)
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Quinoa Tabbouleh

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water or low-sodium broth
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 medium cucumber, diced
  • ½ red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • ¼ cup fresh mint, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • (Optional: ½ cup crumbled feta)

Roasted Vegetables

  • 1 medium zucchini, cubed
  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded & cubed
  • 1 small eggplant, cubed
  • Olive oil, salt & pepper

Recipe Directions

  1. Marinate & Bake Chicken: Combine olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper. Marinate chicken for 30 minutes, then bake or grill until cooked (165°F/74°C) and slice.
  2. Prepare Quinoa & Tabbouleh: Cook quinoa in water or broth. Toss with cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, parsley, mint, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Stir in feta if desired.
  3. Roast Vegetables: Toss zucchini, bell pepper, and eggplant with olive oil, salt, and pepper; roast in a 425°F (220°C) oven for 25–30 minutes until tender and lightly caramelized.
  4. Assemble: Plate sliced chicken over tabbouleh with a side of roasted vegetables. Reserve extra for Tuesday’s lunch.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~500 kcal
  • Protein: ~40 g
  • Carbs: ~40 g
  • Fats: ~18 g

Breakfast: Mediterranean Veggie Omelette
Show/Hide Breakfast Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup spinach, roughly chopped
  • ½ cup diced tomatoes
  • ¼ cup crumbled feta
  • 6–8 Kalamata olives, sliced
  • Fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Recipe Directions

  1. Beat the eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a nonstick skillet; sauté spinach and tomatoes until just wilted.
  3. Pour in the eggs, then sprinkle feta, olives, and basil over top.
  4. Cook until eggs are set and fold the omelette.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~320 kcal
  • Protein: ~22 g
  • Carbs: ~8 g
  • Fats: ~22 g
Lunch: Leftover Monday Dinner
Dinner: Baked Salmon with Olive-Caper Salsa & Roasted Vegetables
(Leftovers for Wednesday Lunch)
Show/Hide Dinner Recipe

Ingredients

Baked Salmon

  • 2 salmon fillets (≈6 oz each)
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • 2 lemon slices (plus extra juice if desired)

Olive-Caper Salsa

  • ¼ cup pitted Kalamata olives, chopped
  • 2 tbsp capers, drained and chopped
  • ¼ small red onion, finely diced
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil

Roasted Vegetables

  • ½ small eggplant, cubed
  • 1 small zucchini, cubed
  • 1 red bell pepper, cubed
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • Olive oil, salt & pepper

Recipe Directions

  1. Bake the Salmon: Brush salmon with olive oil, season with salt and pepper, top with lemon slices, and bake at 425°F (220°C) for 12–15 minutes until flaky.
  2. Prepare the Salsa: Mix olives, capers, red onion, parsley, lemon juice, and olive oil in a bowl.
  3. Roast the Vegetables: Toss eggplant, zucchini, red bell pepper, and cherry tomatoes with olive oil, salt, and pepper; roast until tender.
  4. Assemble: Serve the salmon topped with salsa alongside the roasted vegetables. Reserve extra for Wednesday’s lunch.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~550 kcal
  • Protein: ~35 g
  • Carbs: ~30 g
  • Fats: ~30 g

Lunch: Leftover Tuesday Dinner
Dinner: Mediterranean Chicken Thighs with Chickpea Stew & Couscous
(Leftovers for Thursday Lunch)
Show/Hide Dinner Recipe

Ingredients

Chicken Thighs

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Mixed herbs (thyme, oregano) – 1 tsp each
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Chickpea Stew

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup spinach, roughly chopped
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Couscous

  • 1 cup whole-wheat couscous
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • Chopped parsley – 2 tbsp
  • Salt & olive oil to finish

Recipe Directions

  1. Roast Chicken Thighs: Marinate thighs in olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, herbs, salt, and pepper. Roast at 400°F (200°C) for 35–40 minutes until crispy and cooked through.
  2. Prepare Chickpea Stew: Sauté diced onion in olive oil until translucent. Add garlic, then stir in diced tomatoes, chickpeas, spinach, and smoked paprika. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  3. Make Couscous: Pour boiling water over couscous, cover for 5 minutes, then fluff and stir in parsley, salt, and olive oil.
  4. Assemble: Serve chicken thighs atop the chickpea stew with a side of couscous. Reserve extra stew for Thursday’s lunch.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~600 kcal
  • Protein: ~45 g
  • Carbs: ~50 g
  • Fats: ~20 g
Snack: Baba Ganoush with Pita Chips
Show/Hide Snack Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 medium eggplant
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • Whole-grain pita bread (4 pieces, cut into chips)

Recipe Directions

  1. Roast the eggplant whole at 400°F (200°C) for about 40 minutes until soft. Cool and scoop out the flesh.
  2. Blend the eggplant flesh with garlic, tahini, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper until smooth.
  3. Bake or toast pita pieces until crisp.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~200 kcal
  • Protein: ~5 g
  • Carbs: ~25 g
  • Fats: ~10 g

Breakfast: Avocado Toast with Poached Egg
Show/Hide Breakfast Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup halved cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh basil leaves, a few
  • Salt, pepper & red pepper flakes (optional)

Recipe Directions

  1. Toast the bread.
  2. Mash the avocado with salt and pepper and spread it on the toast.
  3. Poach the eggs in simmering water with a splash of vinegar until the whites are set but the yolks remain soft.
  4. Place a poached egg on each toast, top with cherry tomatoes, basil, and a pinch of red pepper flakes.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~350 kcal
  • Protein: ~14 g
  • Carbs: ~30 g
  • Fats: ~20 g
Lunch: Leftover Wednesday Dinner
Dinner: Greek-Style Fish Stew with Tomatoes & Olives
(Leftovers for Friday Lunch)
Show/Hide Dinner Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 white fish fillets (cod or halibut, ≈6 oz each)
  • 1 medium potato, cubed
  • 1 medium carrot, sliced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 oz)
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • ½ cup Kalamata olives, pitted
  • 1 tbsp capers
  • A splash of white wine
  • Fresh oregano and dill, a few sprigs
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Recipe Directions

  1. Sauté the diced onion and garlic in olive oil until soft.
  2. Add the cubed potato, carrot, and diced tomatoes; simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Add white wine, olives, capers, salt, pepper, and herbs. Gently nestle the fish fillets into the stew and simmer for 8–10 minutes until cooked.
  4. Garnish with extra oregano and dill. Reserve extra for Friday’s lunch.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~500 kcal
  • Protein: ~35 g
  • Carbs: ~40 g
  • Fats: ~15 g

Lunch: Leftover Thursday Dinner
Dinner: Vegetable Moussaka with Lentils & Eggplant
(Leftovers for Saturday Lunch)
Show/Hide Dinner Recipe

Ingredients

Moussaka Layers

  • 2 eggplants, thinly sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 oz)
  • 1 cup cooked lentils
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tsp cinnamon & ½ tsp allspice
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil, as needed

Béchamel Sauce

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 ½ cups low-fat milk
  • A pinch of nutmeg
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Recipe Directions

  1. Prepare Vegetables: Lightly salt the eggplant and zucchini slices; let drain for 15 minutes, rinse, pat dry, then sauté in olive oil until softened.
  2. Make Lentil-Tomato Sauce: Sauté diced onion and garlic in olive oil until translucent. Add diced tomatoes, cooked lentils, cinnamon, allspice, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 15 minutes until thickened.
  3. Prepare Béchamel: In a saucepan, melt butter, whisk in flour until smooth, then slowly add milk while stirring. Add nutmeg, salt, and pepper; cook until the sauce thickens.
  4. Layer & Bake: In a baking dish, layer the sautéed vegetables with the lentil-tomato sauce. Pour the béchamel sauce evenly over the top and bake in a preheated 375°F (190°C) oven for 30–35 minutes until bubbly and golden.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~550 kcal
  • Protein: ~25 g
  • Carbs: ~60 g
  • Fats: ~20 g
