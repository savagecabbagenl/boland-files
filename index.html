Monday

Snack: Veggie sticks (carrot & celery) with a side of classic hummus.
Show/Hide Monday Dinner Recipe (Leftovers for Tuesday Lunch)

Ingredients

Baked Lemon-Herb Chicken

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (6–8 oz each)
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Quinoa Tabbouleh

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water or low-sodium broth
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 medium cucumber, diced
  • ½ red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • ¼ cup fresh mint, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • (Optional: ½ cup crumbled feta)

Roasted Vegetables

  • Assorted seasonal vegetables (zucchini, bell pepper, eggplant), cubed
  • Olive oil, salt & pepper

Recipe Directions

  1. Marinate & Bake Chicken: Marinate the chicken in olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper. Bake or grill until cooked through (165°F/74°C), then slice.
  2. Cook Quinoa & Prepare Tabbouleh: Boil quinoa in water or broth, then mix with tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, parsley, mint, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Roast Vegetables: Toss vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper; roast until tender and slightly caramelized.
  4. Assemble: Serve the chicken over a bed of tabbouleh with roasted vegetables on the side. (Reserve extra for Tuesday’s lunch.)

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~500 kcal
  • Protein: ~40 g
  • Carbs: ~40 g
  • Fats: ~18 g

Tuesday

Breakfast: Mediterranean Veggie Omelette with spinach, diced tomatoes, crumbled feta, Kalamata olives, and fresh basil.
Lunch: Leftover Monday Dinner.
Snack: Apple slices with a handful of raw almonds.
Show/Hide Tuesday Dinner Recipe (Leftovers for Wednesday Lunch)

Ingredients

Baked Salmon

  • 2 salmon fillets (≈6 oz each)
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • 2 lemon slices (plus extra juice if desired)

Olive-Caper Salsa

  • ¼ cup pitted Kalamata olives, chopped
  • 2 tbsp capers, drained and chopped
  • ¼ small red onion, finely diced
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil

Roasted Mediterranean Vegetables

  • Eggplant, zucchini, red bell pepper, and cherry tomatoes
  • Olive oil, salt & pepper

Recipe Directions

  1. Bake Salmon: Brush salmon with olive oil, season with salt and pepper, top with lemon slices, and bake until it flakes easily (12–15 minutes).
  2. Prepare Salsa: Mix olives, capers, red onion, parsley, lemon juice, and olive oil in a bowl.
  3. Roast Vegetables: Toss vegetables in olive oil, salt, and pepper; roast until tender.
  4. Assemble: Plate the salmon with a generous spoonful of salsa alongside roasted vegetables. (Make extra for Wednesday’s lunch.)

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~550 kcal
  • Protein: ~35 g
  • Carbs: ~30 g
  • Fats: ~30 g

Show/Hide Wednesday Dinner Recipe (Leftovers for Thursday Lunch)

Ingredients

Chicken Thighs

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • Olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, mixed herbs, salt & pepper

Chickpea Stew

  • 1 can chickpeas (drained)
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • Spinach, garlic, onion, smoked paprika

Couscous

  • 1 cup whole-wheat couscous
  • Chopped parsley

Recipe Directions

  1. Roast Chicken Thighs: Marinate and roast until crispy and cooked through.
  2. Prepare Chickpea Stew: Sauté garlic and onion, add tomatoes, chickpeas, spinach, and smoked paprika; simmer until flavors meld.
  3. Steam Couscous: Prepare couscous according to package directions and stir in chopped parsley.
  4. Assemble: Serve the chicken thighs over a bed of chickpea stew with couscous on the side. (Reserve extra for Thursday’s lunch.)

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~600 kcal
  • Protein: ~45 g
  • Carbs: ~50 g
  • Fats: ~20 g

Show/Hide Thursday Dinner Recipe (Leftovers for Friday Lunch)

Ingredients

Greek-Style Fish Stew

  • 2 white fish fillets (cod or halibut)
  • 1 potato (cubed) and 1 carrot (sliced)
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • Garlic, onion, olives, capers
  • A splash of white wine
  • Fresh oregano and dill
  • Salt & pepper

Recipe Directions

  1. Simmer Stew: In a large pot, sauté garlic and onion; add potatoes, carrots, and tomatoes with olives, capers, and a splash of white wine. Simmer until vegetables are tender.
  2. Add Fish: Gently place fish fillets in the stew, cook until the fish is just done and flavors meld.
  3. Finish: Stir in fresh oregano and dill, adjust seasoning, and serve. (Reserve extra for Friday’s lunch.)

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~500 kcal
  • Protein: ~35 g
  • Carbs: ~40 g
  • Fats: ~15 g

Show/Hide Friday Dinner Recipe (Leftovers for Saturday Lunch)

Ingredients

Vegetable Moussaka

  • 2 eggplants, sliced thinly
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup lentils (cooked)
  • Cinnamon, allspice
  • Béchamel sauce (light)
  • Olive oil, salt & pepper

Recipe Directions

  1. Prepare Vegetables: Lightly salt eggplant and zucchini slices; roast or sauté until softened.
  2. Make Tomato-Lentil Sauce: Combine diced tomatoes, cooked lentils, cinnamon, allspice, salt, and pepper; simmer until thickened.
  3. Layer & Bake: In a baking dish, layer the vegetables with the tomato-lentil sauce, top with a light béchamel sauce, and bake until golden and bubbling. (Reserve extra for Saturday’s lunch.)

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~550 kcal
  • Protein: ~25 g
  • Carbs: ~60 g
  • Fats: ~20 g
