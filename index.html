Meal 1: Grilled Lemon-Herb Chicken with Quinoa Tabbouleh

Ingredients

For the Chicken

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (≈6–8 oz each)
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

For the Quinoa Tabbouleh

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water or low-sodium broth
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 medium cucumber, diced
  • ½ red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • ¼ cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • (Optional: ½ cup crumbled feta)

Recipe Directions

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper. Coat the chicken and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the Quinoa: Boil water or broth, add quinoa, reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes until absorbed. Let cool.
  3. Prepare the Tabbouleh: In a bowl, combine quinoa, tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, parsley, and mint. Drizzle with lemon juice and olive oil, then season with salt and pepper. Optionally, stir in feta.
  4. Grill the Chicken: Grill on medium-high for 6–7 minutes per side until the chicken reaches 165°F (74°C). Let rest and slice.
  5. Serve: Plate the tabbouleh and top with the sliced chicken.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~500 kcal
  • Protein: ~40 g
  • Carbs: ~40 g
  • Fats: ~18 g

Meal 2: Baked Salmon with Olive-Caper Salsa & Roasted Mediterranean Vegetables

Ingredients

For the Salmon

  • 2 salmon fillets (≈6 oz each)
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • 2 lemon slices (plus extra juice if desired)

For the Olive-Caper Salsa

  • ¼ cup pitted Kalamata olives, chopped
  • 2 tbsp capers, drained and chopped
  • ¼ small red onion, finely diced
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil

For the Vegetables

  • 1 small eggplant, cubed
  • 1 zucchini, half-moons
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1 small red onion, wedges
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, & dried oregano (or basil)

Recipe Directions

  1. Preheat & Roast Vegetables: Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Toss eggplant, zucchini, bell pepper, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and garlic with olive oil, salt, pepper, and oregano on a baking sheet. Roast for 25–30 minutes, stirring halfway.
  2. Bake the Salmon: Place salmon in a greased dish, drizzle with olive oil, season, and top with a lemon slice. Bake for 12–15 minutes until salmon flakes easily.
  3. Make the Salsa: In a bowl, mix olives, capers, red onion, parsley, lemon juice, and olive oil. Adjust seasoning as needed.
  4. Assemble: Spoon the salsa over the salmon and serve alongside the roasted vegetables.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~550 kcal
  • Protein: ~35 g
  • Carbs: ~30 g
  • Fats: ~30 g

Grocery List

  • Proteins: 2 chicken breasts; 2 salmon fillets
  • Produce: 3 lemons; 4 garlic cloves; 1 small eggplant; 1 zucchini; 2 red bell peppers; 1–2 red onions; 2 cups cherry tomatoes; 1 medium cucumber; 1 cup fresh parsley; ¼ cup fresh mint leaves; (optional: extra basil/or oregano)
  • Grains: 1 cup quinoa
  • Pantry: Extra-virgin olive oil; Salt; Pepper; Dried oregano; Capers; ¼ cup pitted Kalamata olives
  • Dairy (Optional): ½ cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Broth (Optional): Low-sodium chicken/vegetable broth
